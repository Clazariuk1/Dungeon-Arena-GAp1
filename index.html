Dungeon Arena

by Christopher Lazariuk

You're an adventurer trapped in a nightmarish dungeon from which there is no escape. Survive the continuous onslaught of enemies. Be warned! Colliding with enemies will damage you, and enemies colliding with the border walls will also damage you!

  • Avoid contact with enemies using the WASD keys
  • Attack by clicking on the enemy boxes
  • Heal yourself with the 'e' key. Costs 20 MP.
  • Lay a bomb in your wake with the 'b' key that detinates after 3 seconds, damaging or destroying all surrounding foes(including you.) Costs 30MP.
  • Annihilate all minor enemies with the 'q' key. Does not affect bosses. Consumes all MP.
  • Freeze Time with the 'r' key and stop enemies in their tracks for five seconds. Costs 75 MP. "COMING SOON."
  • Teleport to your starting position with the 't' key, but don't box yourself into a corner! Costs 50 MP.
